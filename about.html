About Me

I'm a student developer currently in my second year of pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

I specialize in programming robots and building interactive apps and websites, having worked with multiple high-school and university teams such as the RoboJackets and the Drone Delivery Network Project.

Since a young age, I have enjoyed building things, be it tiny Lego robots or elaborate websites for fictional companies. This fascination with 'creating' led me to grow to love the process behind making a product - adequate planning, research, prototyping, collaboration, and testing.

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work on some cutting-edge projects, acquiring valuable technical and interpersonal skills in the process. I hope to continue to build upon my experiences and make meaningful contributions to the teams of passionate individuals that I work with.
